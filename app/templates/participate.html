Open Positions

Graduate Students

Dr. Chang will be accepting graduate students this year to start Fall 2017. Competitive candidates will be highly self-motivated and creative and will possess excellent time-management skills. Applicants will have a background in neuroscience, psychology, cognitive science, engineering, economics, physics, computer science, or applied mathematics and will likely have several years of experience with either quantitative methods (e.g., statistical analysis of fMRI data, data-mining, or machine-learning) or technical computing (e.g., programming preferably C/C++, javascript, python, or matlab; building web applications; databases; high performance computing, etc.). Please contact Dr. Chang for more information before applying.

Research Assistants

The laboratory has several openings for part-time volunteer research assistants to start Fall 2015. Primary responsibilities include assisting in the collection, processing, and analysis of behavioral, psychophysiological, and neuroimaging data. We require a minimum commitment of 10 hours per week for at least one quarter. Please email Dr. Chang for more information.
